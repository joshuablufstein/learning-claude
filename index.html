Built for someone who already uses Claude or ChatGPT regularly and wants to close the gap between using AI well and understanding how it actually works. No coding background needed.

Not a class. A sequence of free resources arranged in the order they make most sense to consume. Each phase ends with a tangible output so you know the gap closed.
